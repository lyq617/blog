ESL Podcast 1165 – Eating Spicy Foods

ESL Podcast 1165 – Eating Spicy Foods

原文

Akim: Oh, this is so good! Why are your eyes watering?

Wendy: It’s so spicy! When you invited me over for curry, I didn’t know it’d be so hot.

Akim: This is mild or maybe medium. I make another version of this that’s much hotter. In fact, I like mine with some extra peppers and some hot sauce. Do you want any?

Wendy: Are you kidding me?! The roof of my mouth is on fire.

Akim: You must have very sensitive taste buds. This has a little kick to it and it’s pungent and zesty, but it’s not too spicy.

Wendy: You must have no taste buds at all if you think this is mild. My mouth needs to cool down from all that heat. I need more water – now!

Akim: All right, but you don’t want to fill up on water. I’ve made a special dessert.

Wendy: Is that supposed to be mild, too?

Akim: No, that really is spicy!

分析

  1. eyes watering：眼泪在打转，然后泪流满面。tears go down your face。
  2. spicy：辣辣辣，火火火。
  3. curry：咖喱粉。a food, dish, or sauce in Indian cuisine seasoned with a mixture of pungent spices。
  4. hot：食物可以hot or cold，表明冷热。也可以mild or hot 说味道。
  5. medium：有点辣，不太辣。
  6. another version of this：说的就是curry food。
  7. Are you kidding me?!：你在逗我吗？（简直不敢相信你说了什么……）
  8. roof of my mouth：上颚
  9. on fire：不是真的fire，是感觉，burning的感觉；on fire说人，表明这个人很精通这个事情，也可以是on hot，比如说一个人打篮球很好，就可以说这个人打篮球on fire/hot。
  10. taste buds：味蕾。
  11. a little kick to it：kick一点点辣，不太辣。
  12. pungent：强烈的味道。
  13. zesty：有点辣的。
  14. to cool down：降温，减少burning的感觉
  15. to fill up on：eat or drink很多很多，就像很久没吃过东西一样。
  16. dessert：饭后甜点

积少成多，每天进步一点点

  1. 很多关于食物味道的表达，尤其是辣和不辣。辣：spicy，hot。然后过渡到：pungent，zesty。最后到：medium，mild。以后在形容食物的时候可就别再只会用delicious了。
  2. 要哭了怎么说，eyes watering很棒的表达，记住哦。eg: I’m eyes watering.
  3. 邀请别人来吃什么怎么说呢？A invite B over for sth. eg: When you invited me over for curry, I didn’t know it’d be so hot.
  4. on fire/hot怎么 用，分析里面都说了啊。上去看！
  5. Are you kidding me?!：你逗我玩吗？
  6. 狼吞虎咽怎么说呢？fill up on food。 eg. All right, but you don’t want to fill up on water.
